The NotGood Ranking System

Here's the full breakdown:

Ranks (total points earned)

▪ Unranked - 0 pts
▪ Bronze - 1-10 pts
▪ Silver - 10-20 pts
▪ Gold - 20-30 pts
▪ Diamond - 30-40 pts
▪ Master - 40pts+

How points work

Personal Performance:
• Every 10 kills = 5pts
• Every 10 assists = 3pts
Team Placement:
• 1st (Win) = 10pts for whole team
• 2nd = 5pts
• 3rd = 3pts
• 4th = 1pt
• 5th-7th = 0pts
• 8th = -2pts
